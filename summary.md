## Methodology
- Cleaned and validated trade and sentiment datasets
- Converted timestamps and aligned data at a daily level
- Created key metrics including daily PnL, win rate, trade frequency, and leverage
- Performed sentiment-based and behavior-based analysis
- Segmented traders by leverage and trading frequency

## Key Insights
- Average daily PnL and win rates vary across sentiment regimes
- Trading activity increases during Fear and Greed periods
- Low-leverage traders show more stable average and median daily PnL compared to high-leverage traders

## Strategy Recommendations
- Reduce leverage to improve performance consistency and manage downside risk
- Adjust trade frequency and position sizing during Fear and Greed regimes to account for higher volatility
